About Us

AurOracle is a group of experts specializing in physics, data analysis, machine learning, space weather, and web development. Our main goal is to use raw data from the DSCOVR satellite to predict geomagnetic storms, even in the presence of intermittent data anomalies. Our secondary mission involves addressing these anomalies in a unique way. In essence, AurOracle team aims to contribute a high-impact solution to advance geomagnetic storm prediction, ultimately enhancing our planet's safety and preparedness.

What is the Problem we are solving and why did we choose this challenge?

We are addressing a complex challenge centered on the severe impact of geomagnetic storms on Earth's infrastructure. The problem primarily concerns the vulnerability of critical systems like power grids, communication networks, and navigation systems to these storms, which can disrupt them significantly. Additionally, anomalies in the "level 2" data from the DSCOVR satellite pose a significant challenge in accurately predicting and preparing for geomagnetic storms. Moreover, there's a lack of awareness about the potential damages caused by these storms. Our goal is to raise awareness, enhance space weather monitoring, and improve disaster preparedness to safeguard our infrastructure and ensure a more resilient future for our planet.

Our Solution

-Predicting Geomagnetic Storms: We use machine learning regression to forecast geomagnetic storms accurately, helping organizations prepare for potential disruptions.
-Fixing DSCOVR Satellite Anomalies: We employ reverse machine learning to rectify anomalies in DSCOVR satellite data, enhancing space weather monitoring.
-Educational Library: We provide a user-friendly online library to educate the public about geomagnetic storms, their history, and safety measures for better preparedness.
